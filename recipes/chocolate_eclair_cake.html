Éclair Cake

Description

This éclair cake is a very quick and simple no-bake dessert. It uses graham crackers and a pudding mixture. It tastes just like an éclair, but there is enough for a crowd! It is a great recipe for children who are learning to cook since there is no baking involved or any power kitchen tools. Just a bowl and a spoon!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk
  • 8-oz. container frozen whipped topping, thawed
  • 2 3.5-oz. packages instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 16-oz. package graham crackers
  • 16-oz. package chocolate frosting

Steps

  1. 1. Gather ingredients.
  2. 2. Stir milk, whipped topping, and pudding mix together in medium bowl until combined.
  3. 3. Arrange a layer of graham crackers in a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  4. 4. Spread half of pudding over crackers, then add another layer of crackers and pudding. Add one more layer of crackers. Cover and place in fridge for 30 minutes.
  5. 5. Spread frosting over cake. Cover and place in fridge for around 4 hours and serve.
