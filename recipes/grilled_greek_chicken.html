Grilled Greek Chicken

Description

The secret to this simple grilled chicken is a very powerful marinade and "roasting" it slowly over semi-indirect heat on the grill.

Ingredients

  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp distilled white vinegar
  • 6 chicken leg quarters
  • 1 lemon, wedges
  • Cavenders greek seasoning to taste
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Steps

  1. 1. Whisk garlic, oregano, red pepper flakes, black pepper, lemon juice, olive oil, vinegar, and Greek seasoning together in large bowl.
  2. 2. Make two slashes on skin side down to the bone in thigh section and one in the leg section of each quarter. Season both sides generously with kosher salt. Thoroughly coat all sides in marinade bowl. Cover and marinate 4 to 12 hours in fridge.
  3. 3. Transfer chicken to paper towel-lined sheet pan to drain.
  4. 4. Place legs on grill, skin side down over semi-direct heat. Cook 6-7 minutes per side. Cook until internal temperature is 165F or 74C, 8-10 more minutes. Serve with lemon.
