French Onion Soup

Description

There's nothing cozier than a warm bowl of freshly made French onion soup. With this top-rated recipe, you can make restaurant-worthy French onion soup in the comfort of your own kitchen. Plus, get our best ingredient tips and storage secrets.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 cups sliced onions
  • 5 cups beef broth
  • 2 tbsp dry sherry
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • Pinch salt and black pepper
  • 4 slices French bread
  • 4 slices provolone cheese
  • 2 slices diced Swiss cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. 1. Gather ingredients.
  2. 2. Melt butter with olive oil in stock pot over medium heat. Add onions to butter and continually stir until tender and translucent. Do not brown onions.
  3. 3. Add beef broth, sherry, and thyme. Season with salt and pepper, then let simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. 4. Preheat oven and set to broil.
  5. 5. Ladle soup into oven-safe serving bowls and place one slice of bread on top of each. Layer each bread slice with provolone slice, half a slice of diced Swiss, and 1 tbsp Parmesan cheese.
  6. 6. Place bowls on baking sheet and broil in oven until cheese bubbles and browns slightly, 2-3 minutes.
